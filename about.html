Meet the Artist!

     


Fethi's life has been a fascinating journey, marked by a deep-seated passion for art and a diverse upbringing that has left an indelible mark on his work. Raised in different regions of Turkey due to his parents' roles as civil servants, Fethi developed a profound appreciation for cultural diversity from a young age.

After completing his education at Bogazici University, Fethi embarked on a successful 35-year career as a software engineer. However, it was a chance encounter with a soldering workshop that led to a transformative shift in his life. Here, his meticulous nature and his fascination with global cultural designs converged, giving birth to the intricate silver jewelry he creates today.

Each piece crafted by Fethi tells a story of his diverse upbringing, meticulous attention to detail, and his deep love for cultural artistry. When not immersed in jewelry-making, Fethi finds joy in playfully interacting with the family cat, experimenting in the kitchen with flavors inspired by his travels, and delving into the fascinating world of sociology. Fethi's journey is a testament to the beauty of discovering one's true passion, and at Fethi's Jewelry, we invite you to explore the world through his eyes, one beautifully detailed piece at a time.
